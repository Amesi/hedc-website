About our clinic

Hope Essence Dental Clinic Limited is a 100% locally-owned Papua New Guinean SME, incorporated in 2021. Our team of qualified national dentists treats oral disease from consultation and diagnosis through to treatment — spanning preventive, restorative, prosthodontic and non-surgical endodontic care, all at an unbeatable price.

“Our mission is to provide comprehensive dental care with personalized treatment plans that meet the unique needs of each patient — exceeding expectations in a warm, welcoming environment.”

Frequently asked questions

How much does a visit cost?
Check-ups start from K150 and every treatment has clear, upfront pricing — we'll always explain the cost before we begin, with no surprises.
Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?
Walk-ins are welcome Monday to Saturday. Booking ahead on WhatsApp guarantees your preferred time and means less waiting.
Do you really remove buai (betel nut) stains?
Yes — it's our specialty. Our deep-clean lifts years of betel nut staining and restores your natural tooth colour, often in a single visit, from K180.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash and common card and mobile transfer options. Ask our reception team when you book and we'll walk you through it.
Do you treat children?
Absolutely. We offer gentle, friendly children's check-ups from K120 and love helping little ones build healthy habits early.
Is a root canal painful?
No. We use modern anaesthesia so the procedure is comfortable — most patients feel little to nothing and are relieved of their toothache.
